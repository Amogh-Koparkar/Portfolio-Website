A 3 poster series on an original play named “Exit.exe”.

The EXIT.exe title combines digital and emotional significance—".exe" suggests a last, executable action, and "Exit" represents an underlying wish to escape sorrow, regret, or unresolved histories. For each of the characters, it is a choice between healing and denial, fantasy and reality. The title asks: when we want an exit, are we in fact exercising freedom—or fleeing?
